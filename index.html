About Me

Full Stack Developer

I am a geologist who is making a career change to become a full stack developer. I graduated from Brigham Young University in 2012 with my BS in Geology and 2014 with my MS in geology. My introduction to coding began in graduate school where I used python and matlab to process large data sets and run statistical analyses on several thousand data points.

As it turns out, I really enjoyed learning to code a little bit. I love to create new things, and to make things work better. I want to do something that I really enjoy, so that I can always be challenged and engaged in my work.

As a professional, I have worked extensively on project teams, am capable of working independently, meeting deadlines, and communicating effectively with team members and clients.

Personal Life
